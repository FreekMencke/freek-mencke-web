OSRS Tracker

(osrs-tracker.freekmencke.com)

I created this web app for Old School RuneScape (OSRS) players to track their progress, keep up with their friends, and even spark some friendly competition.

It has a Price Tracker for checking real-time prices and market trends, along with an XP Tracker that automatically tracks daily XP gains, completed Clue scrolls, Boss kills, and more. You can even add your friends to your favorites to see how they're doing.

The frontend is built with Angular and Tailwind CSS, while the backend runs on AWS Lambda and a MongoDB database.

GitHub: github.com/osrs-tracker
